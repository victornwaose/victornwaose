### 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a frontend engineer and product designer who ships production software for fintech and logistics companies across African markets. I've built and maintained payments products with live native apps, designed and rebuilt company websites end-to-end, and delivered contract work spanning marketing platforms, ad tech, and e-commerce.

I sit at the intersection of engineering and design — I take products from Figma exploration through architecture decisions to deployed, performant interfaces. My work is shaped by the realities of African markets: unreliable networks, low-end devices, and users who can't afford a confusing payment flow.

- 💳 Shipped and maintained **production payments products** — including native mobile apps used by real customers
- 🏗 Architect frontends with **TypeScript, React/Next.js, and Vue/Nuxt** — comfortable owning the full frontend layer of a product
- 🎨 Design in **Figma**, then build it — no handoff loss between design intent and shipped UI
- ⚙️ Work across the stack with **Laravel, Supabase, and PostgreSQL** when the product demands it
- ♿ Treat **accessibility and performance** as requirements, not nice-to-haves
- 🌍 Based in **Asaba, Nigeria** — working with teams globally

### 💡 How I Work

| Principle | In practice |
|---|---|
| 🏦 Fintech-grade rigor | Payment flows can't be "mostly right" — I build with edge cases, retries, and failure states in mind |
| 🎨 Design-engineering hybrid | I move between Figma and code fluently, so nothing gets lost in handoff |
| 📱 Built for real conditions | Low-end devices and 3G networks are my baseline, not an afterthought |
| ♿ Accessibility by default | Semantic markup, keyboard support, and contrast are part of the definition of done |
| 🧹 Code as communication | I write for the engineer who inherits the codebase |
| 🚀 Ship, measure, iterate | Deployed and observed beats perfect and theoretical |
